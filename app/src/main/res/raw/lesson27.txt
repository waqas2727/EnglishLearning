Past Perfect Continuous Tense
Structure: Had + been + past participle:

Example: 
1) I had been finishing my home work for 50 minutes.
2) He had been playing game since morning.
3) He had been drinking milk out the carton when Mom walked into the kitchen.
4) I had been working at the company for five years when I got the promotion.
5) Martha had been walking three miles a day before she broke her leg.
