Future Continuous Tense
Structure: 
Will be + present participle
Am/is/are + going to be + present participle:

Example: 
1) I will be watching the news at 9pm. 
2) I am going to be watching the news at 9pm.
3) I will arrive at five o’clock.
4) Michael will be running a marathon this Saturday.
5) Eric will be competing against Michael in the race.
