Simple Past Tense
Structure: Verb+ed or irregular verb:

Example: 
1) You played the game. 
2) He read the newspaper.
3) Last night I played my guitar loudly and the neighbors complained.
4) She kissed me on the cheek.
5) It rained yesterday.

Past Continuous Tense
Structure: Was/were + present participle:

Example: 1) I was reading newspaper. 
2) He was going to Karachi.
3) The sun was shining every day that summer.
4) As I spoke, the children were laughing at my cleverness.
5) The audience was applauding until he fell off the stage.
